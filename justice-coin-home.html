The utility of Justice Coin is to fund, taking The Human Potential Index to 100% - the most significant objective we can pursue as a collective.

Since the first step is the establishing of appropriate and actual Economic Leadership, Justice Coin facilitates the worldwide transition into the Justice Era, through a process called Economic Leadership Inversion (ELI for short). The vehicles for ELI are Called EcoNational Tokens - cryptocurrencies representing the various groups whose development has been disrupted by colonial activity., from land theft, to enslavement, to genocides. The Justice Coin Protocol mandates that these tokens be introduced in pairs, so as to produce upward pressure that supports both prior and subsequent EcoNational cases. The first pair are The CalTejas, and The Topian, representing the Mexican People, and Blacks of the American Experience.
