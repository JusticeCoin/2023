Whatever You Revalidate, You Perpetuate 


Version 1.0 

Written by 1302

“Whatever you revalidate,you perpetuate.”

-Mark Gregory Dockery-

Nothing new is under the sun - societal problems have persisted since the first Human cities. But while many things get better with time, why is it that we keep doing SOME errors over and over again? What is the mechanism that keeps us persisting in error - entrenching in what we know to be wrong?

One overlooked pathway for this, is revalidation - dog-whistling - virtue signaling - the subtle transmission of the colonial motivation, or the compliance thereof.

It could be as simple as discussing things that don’t matter as if they were more important than they really are. Like how people talk about political theater, or sports, or otherwise produced for TV stuffs. Anything that de-prioritizes Justice, in favor of some comparatively irrelevant distraction. All of that serves to revalidate White Supremacy. But let’s pull out some specific examples for clarity:


Example 1

“So I’ve looked at Justice Coin, but where does the funding come from? Value can’t just be what’s really valuable (the production of Justice) no it has to be some nonsense like production of trinkets, or gold ingots, or the production of bullying etc.”

Why this is Re-Validating White Supremacy:

Why do we bring into question anything not endorsed by white supremacy? The idea that whatever white supremacy deems as valuable is the correct idea, is something we all must be disabused of. Decolonized of, if you will.

One reason people find it hard to see Justice Coin, is because the idea of White supremacy has been SO NORMALIZED in our minds, that we can’t imagine taking our minds back (and with it, our good sense). Gold is just a mineral. The ultimate value is Justice, if not, the idea then is that authority and power belongs to whoever can steal and defend the most gold - that is not reality - and to revalidate another situation, is to promote the disorientation thereof.


Example 2

“I got my vaccine yesterday. Have you gotten yours.”

Why this is Re-Validating White Supremacy:

As if it were an expectation or unspoken commitment every man has taken - a public pledge to take a vaccine being promoted. There is no “MY vaccine”. The cue to verbally take possession of the vaccine (even before you have it), is an extraordinarily powerful revalidation technique. This is used to:

• Attain a response that signals the speaker’s commitment to conform to the values of the questioner.

• Reassure the questioner that he has done the acceptable thing in committing to those values himself

• Decide whether or not the responder is safe or someone that still needs to be converted or attacked.

More to come - I’ll update this topic from time to time. But in the meantime, be careful with the words that come out of your mouth. They are powerful. You manifest what you say, and revalidation is only an indirect, implicit way of saying what is not explicitly being said. You are always going around manifesting the spirit you are led by. If you are led by the spirit (motivation) of white supremacy or conformity to its system - then sister - EVERYTHING YOU DO, and everything you say, is manifesting more white supremacy, and the continuance thereof. And if you are led by Justice as your central motive - your guiding light - them brother - everything you say and do, manifests the Kingdom of God.
